Web Archive restoration

Restored Midnight Sun URLs

The useful historical pages from the old Midnight Sun antique appraisal and estate-sale site have been rebuilt as static, sanitized references.

What was restored

The Web Archive showed a coherent early site about antique appraisals, estate liquidation, estate sales, auction notes, and collectible inventories. Those URLs were stable enough to restore and relevant enough to keep.

Personal contact details, exact street addresses, private biographical details, outdated mail links, old Yahoo scripts, and direct commercial outbound links were removed. Later WordPress articles about unrelated home services, cleaning, waterproofing, removals, garage doors, and similar topics were treated as spam or commercial guest-post content and were not added to the sitemap.
