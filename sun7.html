A privacy-safe historical note preserving the useful inventory categories from an estate sale page.

Inventory Themes

Other categories included bedroom furniture, mirrors, parlor tables, a gumball machine, bar stools, artwork, porcelain, pottery, glass, musical equipment, stereo equipment, housewares, tools, exercise equipment, linens, rugs, and garden items.

Sanitization

The old exact street address and outdated sale timing were removed. The restored page keeps the antique and estate-sale inventory value without exposing old location details.
