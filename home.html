Midnight Sun Auction Company Archive Home

This URL came from the auction-company era of the domain. It now serves as a clean index into appraisal, estate sale, live auction, and inventory resources.

What the old home page told us

The 2010 archive showed a basic home page for an antique auction company with navigation to live auction and photo gallery content. It also contained contact blocks, an old commercial redirect, and hosting-credit links that are not useful to restore.

Useful paths from this era

The useful part is the domain's topic history: antiques, appraisal, estate sale liquidation, auction events, and household collectible inventory. The current restored site keeps that topic and turns the old URLs into guides.

What was removed

The old address, phone numbers, email address, mailto links, and direct commercial outbound links were removed. They are not needed for a useful archive and should not be republished as current contact data.
